Description

Bubble Trap is a 2D action-platformer where players use bubble-based combat mechanics to control the arena. The core concept transforms environmental space into a tactical weapon: instead of direct attacks, players place bubbles strategically to trap enemies inside larger bubbles. Bubble lifetime and scaling limits force quick decision-making. The game loop centers on movement, timing, and arena control—avoid enemy pressure, create bubbles strategically, and collect dropped rewards to increase score and clear levels. Each level features melee and ranged enemies with distinct behaviors, a boss encounter with health visualization, and reward-driven progression toward victory conditions.
