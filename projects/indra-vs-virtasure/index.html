Description

A complete action RPG built in Unreal Engine 5 with sophisticated combat systems, intelligent AI behavior, and deep narrative integration. I directed this project as both Designer and Programmer, architecting the combat loop, AI decision-making, and progression systems from the ground up. The result is a polished, well-balanced experience that earned recognition for its design excellence and technical execution.

Key Accomplishments

  • Architected a combo-based combat system with animation-notified hit detection and dynamic damage scaling
  • Built advanced AI behavior trees with threat evaluation and adaptive difficulty without rubber-banding
  • Designed multi-phase boss encounters with pattern-based state transitions and breakpoint mechanics
  • Implemented quest system with conditional branching and cinematic narrative integration
  • Created character progression with equipment scaling and stat-driven balancing
  • Led iterative playtesting and balance tuning across difficulty curves
  • Organized complex project architecture for rapid design iteration and content pipeline
  • Won 1st and 3rd Prize at CU Project Expo 2025 for design and technical excellence
